Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check my attendance?

You can check your attendance by navigating to the Attendance section in the sidebar. The attendance records are displayed in a table format with dates and status.

How do I view my marks?

Your marks can be viewed in the Marks section. You can filter marks by subject and semester. The marks are displayed along with grades and performance statistics.

How do I contact my teachers?

You can contact your teachers through the Messages section. Click on the Messages link in the sidebar and use the messaging interface to communicate with your teachers.

How do I update my profile?

To update your profile, go to the Profile section and click on the edit button. You can update your personal information, contact details, and profile picture.
